About Me

As a seasoned music engineer with a decade of experience, I bring a wealth of expertise in the realms of sound design and music composition for various mediums including games, film, and TV. Proficient in industry-standard software such as Logic Pro X and WWISE implementation, I possess a Bachelor's of Science in Music Production from Full Sail University, solidifying my foundation in the field. Throughout my career, I've collaborated across diverse genres, honing my skills to cater to the unique needs of each project. Whether it's crafting immersive soundscapes or composing memorable melodies, I am dedicated to bringing your vision to fruition. Let's work together to captivate audiences and breathe life into your creative endeavors. Reach out to me, and let's embark on a journey to transform your dreams into reality.
